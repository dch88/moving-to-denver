Employment in Denver

Tech/Startups

Denver (and Boulder) has an emerging and fast-growing tech scene. Along with more established tech firms like my soon-to-be employer, there is also a growing number of tech startups that offer outstanding employment opportunities.

The best resource I have found for Colorado startups is Built in Colorado

Traditional Employers

Outside of the tech and startup world, Denver also has a large number of traditional employers.

According to choosecolorado.com, some of the top employers in the Denver area are listed below

  • Denver International Airport: 35,000 employees in Denver, CO
  • HealthONE Corporation: 11,050 employees in Denver, CO
  • Lockheed Martin: 14,000 employees in Littleton, CO
  • Centura Health: 8,310 employees in Englewood, CO
  • Comcast Corporation: 8,000 employees in Englewood, CO
